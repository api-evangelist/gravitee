MC, the retail division of Sonae and Portugal’s largest private employer, is undertaking an ambitious technology transformation in retail. As part of a company-wide Tech Transformation program, MC has selected Gravitee as its strategic API management platform — replacing Google Apigee — to unify API governance across the entire organization and lay the architectural foundation for its hybrid multi-cloud, and event-driven future.
