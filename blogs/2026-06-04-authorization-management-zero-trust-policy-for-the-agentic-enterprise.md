Traditional static authorization breaks with AI agents because of their non-deterministic nature. Gravitee's new Authorization Management platform unifies identity and enforces real-time, fine-grained access control across all LLM, MCP, and agent-to-agent interactions, with Catalog-Aware Policy Authoring that binds every authorization policy to a live, centralized inventory of assets.
